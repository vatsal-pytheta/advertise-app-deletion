Overview

For Facebook Login Users

When you sign in to Advertise_App using your Facebook account, we may receive basic profile information (for example: name, email address, and profile ID) strictly to allow you to log in and use app features.

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We only store what is necessary to provide our service.
  • You can request deletion of this data at any time.

Option 1 – Facebook Settings

Recommended

You can remove Advertise_App and its permissions directly from your Facebook account:

1
Open Facebook → Settings & Privacy (or Settings in the app).
2
Go to Apps and Websites and find Advertise_App.
3
Select the app and click Remove . Facebook will disconnect your account and revoke access tokens.
Note: Removing the app on Facebook may not instantly erase data already stored in our systems. For full deletion, use Option 2 below.

Option 2 – Request Data Deletion

Manual Request

To completely delete any personal data that Advertise_App has stored from your Facebook login, please send us a request using the email associated with your Facebook account.

1
Compose an email to support@yourapp.com with the subject: "Facebook Data Deletion Request".
2
In the email body, include:
– Your full name
– Your Facebook account email
– A short message asking for complete deletion of your data from Advertise_App.
3
Once we receive your request, we will locate your account and permanently delete all personal data associated with your Facebook ID from our active systems and backups within 30 days.
